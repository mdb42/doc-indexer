Dr. Elizabeth Blackwell-Smith and Prof. James O'Connor met with Sir Winston Churchill at the Astor-Rockefeller Foundation gala. Mrs. Sarah Van der Meer, accompanied by her husband Lt. Col. Thomas Van der Meer III, discussed medical research with Dr. Marie Curie and Dr. Jonas Salk. Among the other distinguished guests were Pope John Paul II, Queen Victoria's great-granddaughter Princess Margaret, and the renowned composer Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. Rev. Martin Luther King Jr. and Mahatma Gandhi's teachings were frequently cited by Sen. Robert F. Kennedy and President Abraham Lincoln's writings often inspired both Father Damien and Mother Teresa. The event was also attended by philosophers Jean-Paul Sartre and Simone de Beauvoir, while anthropologist Margaret Mead shared insights with psychologist Carl Jung.
